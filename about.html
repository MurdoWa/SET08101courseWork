I've owned this camera since around 2018 (cant remeber the exact date so it may have been eariler) and since then it has been round the world with me so most of the photo you see will have been taken on this photo.

A more recent aqusition of mine this was a very good camera in its day with its 10mp sensor and 4x optical zoom and up to 7x digital zoom. For sentiment reasons this camera will alawys be special to me with its crack in the body and history within my family.
